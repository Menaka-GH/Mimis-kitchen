Pudina Chutney Recipe

Incredients

  • 1.5 teaspoon peanut oil
  • 2 tablespoon urad dal
  • 2 tablespoon toor dal
  • dried red chillies
  • 1 spring curry leaves
  • 1/3 cup fresh shredded coconut
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon jaggery
  • 2 cups mint leaves / pudina leaves
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Method

  • 1. In a pan, add oil and add the dals and red chillies, curry leaves and coconut.
  • 2. Once done cool them down. Add the pudinanin the same pan and fry them until wilted.
  • 3. Grind them all into fine paste.
  • 4. In a pan add oil, when it is hot add mustard and when it splutters, add urad dal and curry leaves.
  • 6. Pour the tadka into the grind chutney.
  • 7. Serve the Pudina chutney idli, dosa.
