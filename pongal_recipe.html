Pongal Recipe

Incredients

  • 1. rice - 1 cup
  • 2. Moong dal - 1 cup
  • 3. Jeera - 1 TSP
  • 4. Pepper - 6 nos.
  • 5. salt - as required
  • 6. water - 4 cups
  • 7. Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp

Method

  • 1. Dry roast moong dal and rice together until aroma comes. Then cool it down.
  • 2. Rinse them atleast 3 times.
  • 3. Add them in the pressure cooker with salt, water, turmeric powder and cook for 3 whistles in the medium flame.
  • 4. Once the gas releases open the cooker and mash the pongal.
  • 5. In a pan add oil, when it is hot add Jeera and pepper, curry leaves.
  • 6. Pour the tadka into the mashed pongal.
  • 7. Serve the pongal with Sambar and Coconut Chutney.
