About me

Hello I am Menaka Hariharasuthan. I am originally from Tirunelveli, Papanasam (TamilNadu, Southern part of India). When I was a young kid I was always interested to work in Kitchen and cooking. Also I used to help my mom to make masala using olden model mortar and pestal which need lot of handwork. My mom always uses freshly ground incredients for the cooking. She always cook freshly for everyday meal. I started learning more and more recipes and soon enough whenever my relatives visited our home for festivals I started cooking for them. I finished my studies in Tamilnadu, India. I did my Bachelor's in Mathematics and did Masters in Computer Applications. I married to Harihara suthan. Now I am in Louisville, Kentucky, USA with my husband and two boys.
