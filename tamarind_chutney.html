Tamarind Chutney Recipe

Incredients

  • 1. Tamarind - 1/2 cup
  • 2. Dates - 1/2 cup
  • 3. Jaggery - 1/2 cup
  • 4. Red chilli powder - 1 tsp
  • 5. Dry Ginger powder- 1/2 tsp
  • 6. Sombu - 1/2 tsp
  • 7. Coriander powder - 3/4 tsp
  • 8. Zeera powder - 1/2 tsp

Method

  • 1. Cook the dates and tamarind with 2 cups of water.
  • 2. When it is cooked cool it down and grind it. Then strain them for debris.
  • 3. Again add it into the pan and keep in low flame.
  • 4. Add chilli powder, sombu, ginger powder, Coriander and Zeera powder
