Upma Recipe

Incredients

  • 1. Rawa (sooji) - 1 cup
  • 2. water - 2 1/4 cup
  • 3. mustard seeds - 1 Tsp
  • 4. salt - as required
  • 5. urad dal - 1/2 Tsp
  • 6. Curry leaves - 5 leaves
  • 7. Channa dal - 1 Tsp
  • 8. Peanuts - 1 Tbsp
  • 9. oil - 1 Tbsp
  • 10. Onions - 1/4 cup
  • 11. Green chilly - 3 no

Method

  • 1. Dry roast the rawa in the pan.
  • 2. In a heavy bottomed pan add oil and when it is hot add mustard seeds. After it splutters, add urad dal, channa dal and peanuts.
  • 3. Once the dal becomes golden color, add the onions, curry leaves and chillies.
  • 4. After the onions become cooked, add the water and let it boil.
  • 5. Then add the rawa litle by little. Make the stove to low flame, so that the rawa wont get lumpy.
  • 6. Mix well while adding rawa and then close with lid for 2 min to cook the rawa.
  • 7. Add 1 tsp of ghee and lemon juice and some chopped coriander leaves.
