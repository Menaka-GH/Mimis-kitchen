Sambar Recipe

Incredients

  • 1. Toor dal - 1 cup
  • 2. Bottle Gourd - 1 cup
  • 3. Shallots - 6 nos.
  • 4. Tomato(medium size) - 3/4 cup
  • 5. Sambar Powder - 1.5 TBSP
  • 6. salt - 1 cup
  • 7. Asafoetida - 2 pinches
  • 8. Turmeric Powder - 1/2 TSP
  • 9. water - 3 cup

Method

  • 1. Pressure cook the dal with water, turmeric and asafoetida
  • 2. In a heavy bottomed pan add oil. Once it is hot add mustard, urad dal. When mustard splutters add curry leaves, chopped onions.
  • 3. Once the onions become translucent, add tomato and fry for 2 mins.
  • 4. Add vegetables and sambar powder and mix well
  • 5. Add water and let it cook. Add tamarind juice also.
  • 6. When the veggies cooked well add the cooked door dal. Add chopped coriander leaves.
