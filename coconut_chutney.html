Coconut Chutney Recipe

Incredients

  • 1. Coconut - 1 cup
  • 2. Fried gram dal - 1/2 cup
  • 3. Green chilly - 3
  • 4. ginger - 1/4 inch
  • 5. salt - as required
  • 6. garlic - 2 pods
  • 7. curry leaves - 6
  • 8. oil - 1 tsp
  • 9. Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp
  • 10. urad dal - 1/2 tsp

Method

  • 1. Cut the coconut into small pieces and add into mixie jar.
  • 2. Add the dal, ginger, garlic, salt, 2 curry leaves and chillies.
  • 3. Grind them into fine paste.
  • 4. In a pan add oil, when it is hot add mustard and when it splutters, add urad dal and curry leaves.
  • 6. Pour the tadka into the grind chutney.
  • 7. Serve the coconut chutney idli, dosa and pongal recipes.
