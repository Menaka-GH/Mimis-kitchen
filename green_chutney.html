Green Chutney Recipe

Incredients

  • 1. Pudina - 1/2 cup
  • 2. Coriander leaves - 1 cup
  • 3. Green chilly - 3
  • 4. ginger - 1/4 inch
  • 5. salt - as required
  • 6. garlic - 2 pods
  • 7. Jeera - 1 tsp
  • 8. lemon juice - 1 tbsp

Method

  • 1. Add the leaves, ginger, garlic, salt, Jeera and chillies.
  • 2. Grind them into fine paste.
  • 3. Add lemon juice while grinding the chutney instead of water.
