Dosa Recipe

Incredients

  • 1. rice - 4 cup
  • 2. urad dal - 1 cup
  • 3. Methi seeds - 1 TBSP
  • 4. salt - as required
  • 5. water - 2 cups

Method

  • 1. Wash the rice, urad dal and methi seeds seperately.
  • 2. Rinse them atleast 3 times. Then soak them over night seperately.
  • 3. Add the methi seeds with the soaked water and grind for a while and then add urad dal and add the water little by little. So that you can get fluffy batter.
  • 4. After 20 mins take the urad dal batter in the pan. Then add rice and little water and grind it until it becomes coarse batter.
  • 5. Then add both the batter and add salt and mix well. After that ferment the batter for 8 hours.
  • 6. Then next day take enough batter and add some water.
  • 7. Once tawa is hot make the dosa and add some oil around and wait for 2 mins, then flip it.
