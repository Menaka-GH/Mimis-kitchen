Mysore Pak

Incredients

  • 1. Sona masuri rice - 2 cup
  • 2. Lemon(medium size) - 3
  • 3. garlic pods - 20
  • 4. Mustard seeds - 2
  • 5. Coriander seeds - 1 tbsp
  • 6. salt - as per taste
  • 7. Jeera - 1 tsp
  • 8. Turmeric Powder - 1/4 TSP
  • 9. oil - 1 TBSP
  • 10. Dry red chillies- 5

Method

  • 1. Soak the Cashews in water and make a puree.
  • 2. In a heavy bottomed pan add oil. Once it is hot add bay leaves, ginger garlic paste.
  • 3. Once the ginger garlic raw smells go away, add the tomato puree, turmeric powder, chilli powder, garam masala.
  • 4. Add water and let the gravy cook until the oil separates.
  • 5. Add the crushed kasuri methi and jaggery.
  • 6. Add the panneer cubes and cook for 2 min. Add chopped coriander leaves.
